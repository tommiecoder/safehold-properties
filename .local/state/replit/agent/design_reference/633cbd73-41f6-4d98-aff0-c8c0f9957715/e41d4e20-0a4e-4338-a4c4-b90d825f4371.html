About Safehold Properties

Founded by a seasoned investment advisor with over a decade of experience in Nigerian real estate, Safehold Properties has become the trusted partner for first-time homebuyers and high-net-worth individuals seeking premium properties.

Our mission is to demystify real estate investment and guide our clients through every step of their property journey, from initial consultation to final ownership. We believe that real estate is not just about buildings—it's about creating foundations for generational wealth.

With a portfolio spanning across Lagos, Abeokuta, Asaba, Abuja, and now South Africa, we've successfully facilitated over ₦50 billion in property transactions, helping our clients build substantial real estate portfolios.
